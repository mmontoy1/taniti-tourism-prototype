Getting to Taniti

Most visitors arrive by air at Taniti International Airport. Cruise ships dock weekly at Yellow Leaf Bay.

Airport Transportation

  • Rental cars available at airport
  • Taxi services available
  • Hotel shuttle (select hotels)

Getting Around the Island

  • Rental cars (most popular option)
  • Public buses run 5am–11pm
  • Car-share services available
  • Walkable areas in Taniti City & Merriton Landing
