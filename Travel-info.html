Currency

U.S. dollars are widely accepted across the island.

Language

English is widely spoken in tourist areas. Some rural residents speak limited English.

Safety

Taniti is generally safe. Petty crime such as pickpocketing occurs in crowded tourist areas. Visitors should take normal precautions.

Holiday Closures

Some attractions and restaurants close on major holidays. Check schedules before planning.

Medical Facilities

Taniti has one hospital and several clinics. Emergency services are available.
