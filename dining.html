Experience the Flavors of Taniti

Taniti offers a wide variety of dining options for families, couples, and travelers. From fresh seafood to international cuisine, there is something for everyone.

🍤 Local Seafood Restaurants

Taniti is known for its fresh, locally caught seafood. Popular options include:

  • Grilled island fish
  • Shrimp and coconut rice
  • Fresh lobster dishes

Many seafood restaurants are located near Yellow Leaf Bay and Taniti City.

🍕 Family-Friendly Dining

Perfect for visitors traveling with children, Taniti offers:

  • American-style diners
  • Pizza and casual cafes
  • Buffet-style hotel restaurants

Most hotels provide on-site dining options for added convenience.

🌎 International Cuisine

Visitors can enjoy a variety of international food options including:

  • Italian restaurants
  • Asian fusion cuisine
  • Mexican grills

🥐 Grocery Stores & Markets

For guests staying in private condos or vacation homes, Taniti has several grocery stores and local markets throughout Taniti City and Merriton Landing.

Note: Most stores close by midnight.

Dining Tips for Visitors

  • Reservations recommended during peak season.
  • U.S. dollars are widely accepted.
  • Some restaurants close on major holidays.
  • Casual dress is accepted in most locations.
